Module description :

Elavon Payment gateway integration for Odoo Website , Ecommerce , Digital Invoice , recurring billing , tokenization and surcharge program option in USA , CANADA , ask us for other available countries. BNPL(BuyNowPayLater) integrated to checkout as an option.

Overview :

The Elavon Payment Integration for Odoo module seamlessly connects Elavon processing with the Odoo Website and Ecommerce Checkout, also integrates with Odoo invoicing/accounting apps which allows you to send an invoice directly from Odoo billing with a hosted payment link. This integration ensures that businesses’s can process transactions efficiently and securely directly through their Odoo interface and check out process, leveraging the payment processing capabilities of Elavon. The Surcharge Program is also an option that can be added for website and also any other creditcard transaction.

Direct Integration :

Direct Connection : Integrate Elavon payment Gateway directly with Odoo Website/Ecommerce or invoicing without the need for third-party processor’s.

Realtime Payments : IProcess payments in real-time, ensuring quick and efficient transaction handling.

Customizable and for all sizes :

* Customize your gateway settings to match your business requirements, including business type, multiple currency, Applepay , Googlepay , Paypal etc..

* For businesses of all sizes, from small retail websites, to gyms with recurring billing to large enterprises with multiple Employee ID and payment methods.

Features :

Automatic Reconciliation : Automatically reconcile payments in Odoo.

Refund Processing : Easily process refunds , void directly through the Odoo system.

Billing and invoicing : Create a digital invoice with Odoo and email to customer with Elavon hosted payment link.

Pay Button : accept payments on nearly any website or via email.

AReports : get all your payment reports set as you want from Odoo reporting.

ASurcharge program: Pass on CreditCard Fees to CardHolders , card holder choice of 0$ for debit transactions.

Auth & capture: preauthorize a transaction on your website and capture it at different amount later. Example transaction comes authorized through website check out , capture or close transaction manually once your done adding shipping costs . or fully automate the sale with shipping included at checkout process.

Account updater: Automatically update expired creditcard accounts.

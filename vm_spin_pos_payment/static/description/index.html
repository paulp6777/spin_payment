Module description :

DejaVoo Pos Payment terminal integration for Odoo with surcharge program and DualPricing/CashDiscount options in USA , CANADA , ask us for other available countries.

Overview :

The Spin POS Payment Integration for Odoo module seamlessly connects DejaVoo.io payment terminals with the Odoo Point of Sale (POS) system. This integration ensures that businesses can process transactions efficiently and securely directly through their Odoo POS interface, leveraging the payment processing capabilities of Nuvei.

Direct Integration :

Direct Connection : Integrate Dejavoo.io payment terminals directly with Odoo POS without the need for third-party processors.

Realtime Payments : Process payments in real-time, ensuring quick and efficient transaction handling.

Customizable and for all sizes :

* Customize Pos settings to match your business requirements, including business type, receipt printing options, etc..

* For businesses of all sizes, from small retail stores with 1 terminal to large enterprises with multiple terminals.

Features :

Automatic Reconciliation : Automatically reconcile payments in Odoo.

Refund Processing : Easily process refunds directly through the Odoo POS system.

Tips : Easily set up tips to the table payment for restaurants with the Z9 ,4G/Wifi wireless model.

Dual pricing : available for USA only , option to bill on the creditcard fees to card holder and cover your creditcard processing fees to 0$

Reports : get all your payment reports set as you want from Odoo reporting.

Surcharge program: Pass on CreditCard Fees to CardHolders , card holder choice of 0$ for debit transactions.

Auth & capture: preauthorize a transaction on your website and capture it at different amount later. Example transaction comes authorized through website check out , capture or close transaction manually once your done adding costs.
